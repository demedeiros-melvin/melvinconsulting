Marketing & Brand Consulting Services for Creators and Small Businesses

MAD Obsidian Consulting is a creator marketing consultant studio delivering practical marketing systems for small teams. We focus on content systems, website optimization, and light paid ads that fit your calendar and budget. Every engagement includes collaborative sessions, clean docs, and a handoff you can actually use. We work with creators, solopreneurs, and small business marketing leads who want proactive guidance without a bloated retainer. Expect clear timelines, weekly check-ins, and specific recommendations rather than generic decks. Everything is scoped to run on the tools you already know.

We sequence projects so you get quick wins first—refresh the offer, clean up analytics, then layer in campaigns—before moving into ongoing support.

Content System Setup for Creators

We act as your creator marketing consultant to design a weekly publishing rhythm that survives real life. We analyze what’s already resonating, define content pillars, and build templates for scripts, captions, and thumbnails. Small dashboards show saves, replies, and clicks so you know what to repeat. These are content systems you can delegate or run yourself without adding another platform to babysit.

Website & Landing Page Design

Your website should make it obvious what you offer and how to book. We craft focused landing pages and mini-sites with sharp copy, offer stacks, and lead capture. Website optimization comes standard: metadata, search-friendly structure, analytics, and basic speed checks. Each handoff includes a Loom walkthrough so small business marketing teams can edit confidently between launches.

Paid Ads Setup & Light Management (Meta, TikTok, Google)

We set up paid ads that support your organic content instead of replacing it. That means clean tracking, sensible campaign structures, and weekly creative swaps across Meta, TikTok, or Google. We stay honest about results—no growth promises—while giving you tidy reports, budget views, and a simple testing plan. It’s a light management layer that plugs into your existing marketing systems.

Brand Messaging & Positioning

Brand consulting should clarify, not complicate. We run collaborative workshops to capture audience language, outline proof points, and decide what you stand for. From there we write messaging pillars, headlines, and social bios that keep your content strategy and website consistent. Everything is documented so future scripts, emails, and ads start from the same voice.

Simple Funnel & Lead Capture Systems

Funnels don’t have to be complex to work. We map the path from your content systems to a booking link or purchase page, then wire forms, tags, and follow-ups through tools you already use. Simple automation keeps leads organized while letting you reply with personality. Each build leaves room for future website optimization or paid traffic without rebuilding the stack.

Not sure where to start?

Tell us about your goals, and we’ll recommend the first marketing systems to tackle—whether that’s a content refresh, landing page cleanup, or light ad testing.
