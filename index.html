Marketing & Business Services for Creators, Small Businesses, and E-Commerce Brands

MAD Obsidian Consulting builds marketing, content, and operations systems for creators, small businesses, and e-commerce brands. We keep deliverables simple to manage—ads, websites, content, and backend processes that your team can run without added complexity.

Advertisement Creation + Management

Full-funnel campaigns across Meta, Google, TikTok, and LinkedIn. We design creative, set up tracking, and manage weekly optimizations so your spend stays efficient.

  • Pixel installation and conversion tracking
  • Creative development, targeting, and A/B tests
  • Weekly reporting with clear next steps

Website Creation & Optimization

Fast, modern sites that convert visitors. We design, build, and optimize pages with clean copy, SEO basics, analytics, and optional AI chatbots for customer support.

  • Custom design, responsive layouts, and speed checks
  • On-page SEO, analytics, and clear CTAs
  • CMS training so your team can edit quickly

Content Creation & Clipping

Consistent content for social, YouTube, and podcasts. We produce long-form pieces and short-form clips tailored for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

  • Video production and podcast editing
  • Short-form clipping, thumbnails, and graphics
  • Scheduling, publishing, and performance tracking

Supply Chain Optimization & Management

For e-commerce and product brands that need reliable operations. We streamline inventory, vendors, logistics, and fulfillment to cut costs and improve delivery.

  • Inventory systems and vendor coordination
  • Logistics, fulfillment, and order tracking
  • Process documentation and cost reduction plans

Custom Business Solutions

Tailored projects for unique needs—automation, CRM setup, email marketing, analytics dashboards, or anything else that keeps your operations running smoothly.

  • Automation, CRM, and email marketing setup
