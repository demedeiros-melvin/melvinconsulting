About MAD Obsidian Consulting

MAD Obsidian Consulting helps creators, founders, and small businesses build sustainable marketing systems through content, websites, and light automation. We keep retainers small and timelines clear so you know exactly what’s being built and why. Our team blends strategy sessions, lightweight research, and build days to deliver content systems, website optimization, and simple funnels you can maintain. We care about honest reporting, documentation, and making sure every channel points to a measurable next step.

How We Work

We start with an audit to understand your current marketing systems, then co-build a strategy that prioritizes the fastest wins. Implementation days focus on content planning, website updates, and connecting tools so your team can run the playbook without heavy oversight.

After the first launch, we refine what’s live by reviewing analytics, scraping audience language, and tightening copy. No bloated retainers—just clear scopes, transparent timelines, and assets that are easy to maintain.
