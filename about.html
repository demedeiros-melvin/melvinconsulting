Our story

Our Story

In geology, obsidian is born from chaos—molten rock that cools into glass sharp enough to cut. We embrace that paradox: the heat of competition and the pressure of growth create clarity and strength.

Like volcanic glass, our strategies are forged under fire and built to stay sharp. We turn messy inputs—content ideas, product shifts, analytics noise—into precise systems that can take a hit and keep working.

MAD Obsidian began when our founder set out to harness transformation itself, forging ideas into structures that endure. That spirit guides every engagement: build fast, document obsessively, and leave you with tools you can wield long after we leave the room.

How We Work

We start with an audit to expose friction across your marketing systems, then co-build a plan that prioritizes the fastest wins. Implementation days focus on content sprints, page updates, and wiring the right tools so your team can operate without heavy oversight.

After launch, we tighten what’s live—reviewing analytics, scraping audience language, and refining copy. No bloated retainers, just clear scopes, transparent timelines, and assets that keep their edge.

Founder Bio

MAD Obsidian is led by a data-driven, systems-minded founder who enjoys working alongside creators and small business owners. Projects blend creative direction with operations, ensuring every campaign ships with a checklist, a testing plan, and documentation your team can keep using.
