Our story

Our Story

We started MAD Obsidian to help creators, small businesses, and e-commerce brands cut through the noise. We believe great marketing doesn't need to be complicated—it needs to be clear, practical, and built to last.

That's why we create simple systems you can actually use, not fancy strategies that sit in a folder. We work quickly, document everything clearly, and give you tools you can use yourself long after our project ends.

Who We Work With

Creators & influencers

Systems that keep you publishing—hooks, content batching, and scripts on your schedule.

Small business owners & coaches

Clear offers, more bookings, and personal follow-up that stays simple.

E-commerce brands & product teams

Marketing and operations support paired with inventory, fulfillment, and customer service needs.

How We Work

We start with a review to find what's not working in your marketing, then create a plan with you focusing on quick wins. Work days focus on content creation sessions, page updates, and connecting the right tools so your team can run things independently.

After launch, we improve what’s live—reviewing analytics, learning how your customers interact, and refining copy. No expensive long-term contracts, just clear projects, transparent timelines, and materials that keep working.
